Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Beef
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Dice Carrots and celery
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saute veggies
  3. When soft add the beef until cooked
  4. Add tomato sauce
  5. Layer into pan with noodles and add cheese on top
